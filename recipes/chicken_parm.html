Chicky pahm

A chicken parm above

This was my favorite dish as a teenager

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Provologne

Steps

  1. Cover chicken with bread crumbs
  2. air fry chicken
  3. Heat up Sauce
  4. Pour a little bit of sauce on chicken
  5. Put cheese on chicken and let cook
  6. Once done cooking, pull out of air fryer and enjoy!
