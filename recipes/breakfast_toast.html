Breakfast Toast

A breakfast toast above

A great and healthy way to start your day!

Ingredients

  • Multigrain Bread
  • One Banana
  • Peanut Butter
  • Optional: Honey

Steps

  1. Toast Bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on toast
  3. Cut a banana into slices and stick on toast
  4. Drizzle some honey on it!
