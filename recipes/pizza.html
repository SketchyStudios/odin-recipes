Pepperoni Pizza

A Pepperoni Pizza above

This is a pretty easy recipe that any college kid can make

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pepperoni
  • Cheese
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Italian Herbs and Seasoning

Steps

  1. Roll Out Pizza Dough
  2. Put Sauce on pizza dough
  3. Sprinkle with cheese
  4. PLace Pepperoni on
  5. Bake at 375F for about 18 minutes
  6. Hit it with the italian herbs and seasoning after you pull it out of the oven
